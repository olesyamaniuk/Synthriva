ABOUT SYNTRIVA

About Us

Synthriva is a dream-to-animation studio powered by artificial intelligence. We give life to the untold stories of your subconscious by turning dream descriptions into emotionally resonant, stylized short films. Whether abstract, surreal, or symbolic — each dream becomes a visual journey shaped by personalized generative tools.

How It Works

  • 1

    Submit Dream Description

    Write a dream as you remember it — fragmented, poetic, or detailed.

  • 2

    Choose Mood & Style

    Match your dream with an aesthetic: dreamy, glitchy, pastel, dramatic, cosmic.

  • 3

    AI Deconstruction

    The system identifies scenes, characters, metaphors, settings, and mood changes.

  • 4

    Story Reconstruction

    A dramaturgy algorithm reconstructs a visual narrative in 6–12 scenes.

  • 5

    Render & Review

    The animation is generated in under 5 minutes. Optionally adjust style or sequence.

  • 6

    Download or Share

    You receive an MP4 or private link to your cinematic dream.

FAQ

  • How long does it take to generate a dream film?
    ~1–5 minutes depending on length and complexity.
  • Can I edit the animation after it’s generated?
    Basic edits like reordering scenes or switching styles are available.
  • Are the videos private?
    Yes, your dreams are confidential by default unless you choose to share them.
  • Can I use my animation commercially?
    Yes, under personal or non-commercial license. Commercial rights are negotiable.
  • What kind of dreams work best?
    Any – abstract, nightmarish, romantic, absurd – the more expressive, the better.
  • What visual styles are available?
    Surrealist, Neon Vaporwave, Dreamcore, Ink & Watercolor, Soft Pastel.
  • Can I narrate my dream?
    Yes, AI voice-over or user recording options are available.
  • Is this therapy?
    No. Synthriva is a creative and artistic experience, not a medical service.
